Year in Review

Message from CEO

A Message to Shareholders

Throughout the year, Nova Group improved operating processes, upgraded technology capabilities and expanded collaboration with partners across key sectors to support a rapidly changing market.

We remain focused on sustainable, transparent and accountable growth, balancing performance, social responsibility and long-term shareholder value.

Company Overview

Company Overview

Nova Group operates across enterprise solutions, digital services and partner network management, connecting data, technology and professional teams to deliver reliable and scalable services.

Vision To be a leading organization growing through innovation and responsibility.
Mission To develop easy-to-use, high-quality services that create measurable outcomes.
Business Scope Digital platforms, customer services, enterprise solutions and business support systems.

Business Performance

Performance by Business Unit

The company reports across three operating groups to provide a clear view of revenue, margin quality and future growth opportunities.

Digital Solutions Revenue: THB 4,280 million

Growth was driven by enterprise adoption of online platforms and long-term service contract renewals.

Enterprise Services Revenue: THB 3,950 million

The business expanded across financial services, retail and logistics customers with stronger after-sales support.

Partner Network Revenue: THB 2,600 million

Regional partner coverage improved, creating better service consistency and stronger operating leverage.
